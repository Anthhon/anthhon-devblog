## Hey, I'm Anthony

I'm a Computer Science student at **UFC — Universidade Federal do Ceará**, based in Fortaleza, Brazil.

I'm obsessed with understanding how computers *really* work — not just writing code that runs, but knowing *why* it runs and what the machine is actually doing with it.

My main interests are:

- **Low-level programming** in C and assembly
- **Reverse engineering** — reading and understanding compiled binaries
- **Systems programming** — memory management, OS internals, compilers
- **Writing and teaching** — making hard topics accessible in Portuguese

## Why This Blog

There's a huge gap in Brazilian Portuguese technical content for low-level programming. Most tutorials stop at "here's how to write a for loop." I want to go further — explain what the linker does, what happens to your stack when you call a function, how GDB actually works.

This blog is where I document my learning, publish tutorials, and share drafts from my book **Descompilando**.

## The Book

I'm writing **Descompilando — Do código da máquina para o ser humano**, a free book in Portuguese covering C programming from first principles all the way to reading and understanding machine code. Check the [book page](/book/) for details and downloads.
